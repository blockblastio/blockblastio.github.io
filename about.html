Our Story

Block Blast started as a passion project inspired by classic games like Tetris. We wanted to make logic puzzles accessible and exciting for everyone, adding our own twist with relaxing gameplay and fun challenges. Today, Block Blast is enjoyed by thousands of players worldwide.
